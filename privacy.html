What data do we collect?

Team SLICK do not collect any personal data through this website or through the ARt: Leeds app. The app requires permission to access the device's location to allow you to find designated ARt walls near to you. Your location is never stored by us. The augmented reality functionality of the app requires permission to use the device's camera. Data from your camera is never stored by us. If you experience an ANR (application not responding) and choose to submit a crash report through Google Play, that report will be anonymised before it is available to us.

Marketing

We do not send out any marketing communications.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Team SLICK do not use cookies on this website or in the ARt: Leeds app.

Privacy policies of other websites

The Team SLICK website may contain links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

Team SLICK keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 11 August 2019.
